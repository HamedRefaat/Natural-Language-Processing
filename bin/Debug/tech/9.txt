One hedge fund's plan to fix Yahoo: Fire 9,000 - and Marissa Mayer too. An activist shareholder is calling for Yahoo to radically change its strategy, fire CEO Marissa Mayer and even revert to its old logo.
Eric Jackson, managing director of SpringOwl Asset Management, a New York-based hedge fund, has sent Yahoo's board a scathing review of the company's management. In a 99 slide presentation, Jackson detailed his plan to turn Yahoo around.
