UFC Featherweight World Champion Conor McGregor may have to wait until at least June 2016 to defend his title.The Crumlin star heads a long list of injuries to come out of UFC 194, McGregor injured his wrist in his 13 second victory over reigning champion Jose Aldo Junior on Sunday.
According to the report he now ‘requires x-ray of left wrist, if positive then must be cleared by doctor or no contest until 06/12’.
