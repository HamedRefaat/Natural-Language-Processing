At London 2012, Pakistani swimmer Anum Bandey achieved her ambitious goal of trimming a whole three seconds off her country’s 400m medley record. In the latest instalment of our “Words of Olympians” series, she recalls her Olympic experience.In taking to the starting block for the semi-final of the women’s individual 400m medley at London 2012, Anum Bandey became only the third female Pakistani swimmer in history to compete in the Olympics, following in the footsteps of Rubab Raza at Athens 2004 and Kiran Khan at Beijing 2008.
