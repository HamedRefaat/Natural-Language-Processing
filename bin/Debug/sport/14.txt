Number one 3x3 player in the world Dusan Domovic Bulut was named 2015 FIBA 3x3 All Stars MVP after leading Novi Sad Al Wahda (UAE) to another title and topping all scorers at the event in Doha, Qatar on 11 December 2015.After earning MVP honours at the Abu Dhabi on 15-16 October 2015, Bulut once again stood out, scoring 27 points, including 5 two-pointers.The Serbian cross-over specialist was instrumental in his team's win in the final and scored the winning free throw, which sealed Novi Sad Al Wahda's back-to-back at the FIBA 3x3 All Star.
