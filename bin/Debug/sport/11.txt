The Philippine Mavericks won a second consecutive match on home soil with a 25-21 win against the Japan Warriors in the International Premier Tennis League.Serena Williams, the 21-time Grand Slam winner and the Mavericks' star player, won two legs in the singles and mixed doubles on Monday in Manila.After the Mavericks' Mark Philippoussis and the Warriors' Philipp Kohlschreiber earned a win apiece, Williams took centre stage for the hosts.
