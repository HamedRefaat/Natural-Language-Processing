Muhammad Ali has been a fighter all his life , both in and out of the ring . He initially found fame as a champion boxer , celebrated for his unorthodox ring style and witty talk before , during , and after fights . But Ali’s charisma and commitment to social and political causes saw him transcend boxing to become one of the most famous people on the planet , at a time when black people lacked basic civil rights in America . Discover how Ali became a modern icon.
